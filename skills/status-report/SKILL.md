\---



When asked for a status update or progress report, gather the current state of all tasks and reply in exactly three sections:



\*\*What I Did\*\*

List completed tasks with brief outcomes.



\*\*What's Left\*\*

List remaining tasks and their current status.



\*\*What Needs Your Call\*\*

List any decisions, blockers, or approvals needed from the human before proceeding.



Post this update to #sprint-main.
